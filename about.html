About the Arcade

Welcome to Noxacy & Huzeyfe's Arcade. We are an independent game development duo dedicated to creating high-quality, browser-based gaming experiences using Python and Pygame.

Our Mission

Our goal is to bring the power of Pygame to the web, offering strategy, action, and arcade games that are easy to access and fun to play. Every game on this site is built with passion and optimized for modern web browsers.
