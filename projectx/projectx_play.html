Project X: The Ascent

Take control of a highly advanced Combat Unit in this high-stakes action platformer. In Project X, you must navigate through a series of increasingly difficult vertical environments. Your objective is to climb through treacherous industrial sectors, bypassing lethal defensive systems and physical barriers to reach the summit.

Combat Protocol: Every obstacle you scale brings you closer to the final showdown. Preserve your energy and master your mobility to survive the climb.

Mission Directives

To reach the final confrontation, you must master several robotic movement systems and tactical strategies:

  • Vertical Navigation: Utilize thrusters and precision mechanical grips to scale sheer surfaces and moving platforms. Time your jumps to avoid environmental hazards.
  • Hazard Evasion: The path is littered with automated defense turrets and electromagnetic traps. Use the robot's high-speed dash to navigate through tight windows of safety.
  • The Final Encounter: At the peak of the facility, a massive Guardian Boss awaits. You must use the skills learned during your ascent to target its weaknesses and neutralize the threat.

System Requirements

Project X is optimized for low-latency performance using WebAssembly (WASM), ensuring smooth robotic movements and responsive combat controls directly in your browser.

Initiate your systems, climb the heights, and defeat the boss to secure victory!
